As you navigate through the corrupted dimensions of the Upside Down, you encounter Joyce Byers, the fierce mother who never gave up searching for her son. Her determination to protect Will and her unwavering resolve in the face of impossible odds have made her a guardian of the foundation. The path to reaching her was filled with trials, but your perseverance and skill have brought you here. Joyce acknowledges your determination and grants you access to the protected realm.
